Akan Names

The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living in the southern regions of present-day Ghana and Ivory Coast in West Africa.The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
This app takes in your birth day and tells you your Akan name depending on your gender.
